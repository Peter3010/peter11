Kenyan Mandazi

Ingredients (makes 20)

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp cardamom powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Oil for deep frying

Traditional Method

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. Gradually add coconut milk to form soft dough
  3. Knead for 5 minutes, then rest for 30 minutes
  4. Roll out and cut into triangles or circles
  5. Fry in medium-hot oil until golden brown
  6. Drain on paper towels

Serving Tradition

Best enjoyed with chai for breakfast or as an evening snack. A staple in Kenyan households.
