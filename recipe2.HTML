Traditional Githeri

Ingredients (serves 6)

  • 2 cups maize (corn)
  • 2 cups beans (preferably red kidney)
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Cooking Process

  1. Soak maize and beans overnight
  2. Boil until tender (about 2 hours)
  3. In separate pan, fry onions until golden
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft
  5. Mix with boiled maize and beans
  6. Simmer for 15 minutes

Cultural Significance

Githeri is a complete protein meal that sustained many Kenyan communities. Often eaten with avocado or kachumbari.
